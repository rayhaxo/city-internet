FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting connected.

Still have questions?
Is installation really free?
Yes! Installation is completely free for all plans. The only exception is if your location requires an unusually long cable run — in that case, we'll discuss the cable cost with you transparently before starting. No surprises.
What is XPON technology?
XPON is a next-generation passive optical network technology that combines EPON and GPON standards. It delivers fiber-speed internet directly to your home with extremely low latency and high reliability — far superior to old cable or DSL connections.
How long does installation take?
Typically 2–4 hours from arrival. Our technicians handle everything — cable routing, ONU device installation, router setup and speed testing. Most customers are online the same day they apply.
Are there any data limits or fair usage policies?
No data caps. All our plans are truly unlimited — you can stream, download, game and video call as much as you want without any throttling or extra charges.
Do I need to buy my own router?
You need an ONU device (we supply from ৳650) which connects to our fiber line. For WiFi coverage inside your home, we also supply routers from top brands like TP-Link, Cudy, Mercusys and more. We'll recommend the best option for your home size.
What happens if my internet goes down?
Call or WhatsApp us anytime — we're available 24/7. Our technicians aim to resolve most issues remotely within minutes, and if an on-site visit is needed, we dispatch someone as quickly as possible.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade (or downgrade) your plan at any time. Just contact us and we'll process the change, usually effective from your next billing cycle at no extra charge.
